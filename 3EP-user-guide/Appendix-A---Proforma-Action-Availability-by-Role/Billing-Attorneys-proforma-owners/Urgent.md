### **Urgent**

Selecting the **Urgent** category displays all proformas for which the user is the owner, which are not submitted yet and the due date has passed.

Proformas with the following statuses are available:

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 11%" />
<col style="width: 24%" />
<col style="width: 64%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><strong>Status</strong></th>
<th><strong>Sub-status</strong></th>
<th><strong>Description</strong></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="5"><strong>Urgent</strong></td>
<td>-</td>
<td>Proformas in this status have met or surpassed their urgency date.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Rejected</td>
<td>Proformas in this status have been rejected by the approver and returned to the billing attorney. Their urgency date has been met or surpassed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Returned</td>
<td>Proformas in this status have been returned to the billing attorney. Their urgency date has been met or surpassed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Biller returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after they were already submitted, and the biller approves/makes specified edits and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney. Their urgency date has been met or surpassed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Completed</td>
<td>Proformas in this status have been already submitted by the billing attorney and are waiting for approver review. Their urgency date has been met or surpassed.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
