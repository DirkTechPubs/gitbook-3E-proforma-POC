### **My proformas**

Selecting the My proformas category displays all proformas for which the user is the owner, and which are not yet submitted.

Proformas with the following statuses are available:

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 12%" />
<col style="width: 12%" />
<col style="width: 75%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><strong>Status</strong></th>
<th><strong>Sub-status</strong></th>
<th><strong>Description</strong></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="4"><strong>Needs review</strong></td>
<td>-</td>
<td>New proformas that have not been opened or edited.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Rejected</td>
<td>Proformas rejected by an approver and returned to the billing attorney for further review.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after it was already submitted and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Biller returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after it was already submitted and the biller approves/makes specified edits and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="4"><strong>In review</strong></td>
<td>-</td>
<td>Proformas opened and/or edited by the user.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Rejected</td>
<td>Proformas rejected by an approver and returned to the billing attorney and were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after they were already submitted and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Biller returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after it was already submitted and the biller approves/makes specified edits and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="4"><strong>Urgent</strong></td>
<td>-</td>
<td><p>Proformas in this list have met or surpassed the calculated “due date”.</p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong>Urgency date is calculated based on a setting in 3E Override / Set System Options and the proforma date.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Rejected</td>
<td>Proformas rejected by an approver and returned to the billing attorney and the urgency date has been met or surpassed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after it was already submitted and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Biller returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after it was already submitted, and the biller approves/makes specified edits and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney. Their urgency date has been met or surpassed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>Approval pending</strong></td>
<td>-</td>
<td>Proforma is waiting to be reviewed by an approver</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
