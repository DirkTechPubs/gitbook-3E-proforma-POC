## Advantages of 3E Proforma

The advantages of 3E Proforma include:

- Users can collaborate with other timekeepers for review or approval.

- Users can provide basic instructions for transfers, write-ups/downs without needing to learn new terminology.

- Reduces (sometimes eliminates) paper used in the proforma process as well as shipping and overnight delivery charges associated with routing proformas to mobile staffers and branch offices.

- Edited proformas can be returned to accounting immediately, expediting the process of turning proformas into deliverable invoices and improving cash flow.

- Email notification can be sent to a timekeeper when a proforma is sent to them for review.
