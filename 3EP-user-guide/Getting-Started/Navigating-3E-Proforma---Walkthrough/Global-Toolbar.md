### **Global Toolbar**

The Global toolbar displays always at the top of the 3E Proforma app, in both the Proforma List and Proforma Detail view. It contains two critical options:

- Working as

- Notifications

- Resource Center

<img src="../../3EP-UG-img/media/image20.png" style="width:6.5in;height:1.9375in" />

#### Working As

The **Working as** field displays who the logged-in user is working as. By default, the “Working as” timekeeper is the default timekeeper as designated in the User / Role Management setup in 3E. You can switch between the timekeepers you are “working as” based on the timekeepers mapped to you in 3E User / Role Management.

#### Notifications 

The Notification bell icon provides a visual indicator to notify you if any print or preview jobs are complete and ready for viewing. Click the **Notification** icon for details.

#### Resource Center 

Click to open the 3E Proforma Resource Center for announcements and What's New information.
