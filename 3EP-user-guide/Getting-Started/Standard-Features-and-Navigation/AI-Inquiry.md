# AI Inquiry

## Elite Intelligence

3E Proforma provides an AI solution called Elite Intelligence which is imbedded within the billing workflow. Elite Intelligence enables you to query the 3E database for WIP, AR balances, payments, statement history, rates, and budgets directly in 3E Proforma. Additionally, this AI solution can be used to create AI-driven narratives and PTA code suggestions, streamlining time card entry.

This AI solution, using a 3E API integration, also enables you to ask questions about a specific proforma in the AI Inquiry instead of having to leave 3E Proforma to search for the information on the proforma.

This feature reduces manual effort and minimizes errors by intelligently recommending appropriate codes and descriptions based on context. By automating part of the entry process, users save time and can focus more on reviewing and refining their proformas rather than searching for the right codes. This feature is especially helpful for users managing high volumes of time entries or working across multiple matters.

With Elite Intelligence, attorneys and billing teams will become more self-sufficient, reducing reliance on back-office staff and shortening billing cycles, reducing bottlenecks, and accelerating approvals.

This AI solution can also be used to [delegate common actions](AI-Inquiry.md#delegate-tasks-with-ai-for-proforma), reducing manual effort and improving efficiency.

Elite Intelligence is accessible in both the Proforma List and Proforma Detail views. Click the **Elite Intelligence** button at the bottom right-side of the page to access the query pop-up.

**Proforma List**

Inquiry is available in the Proforma List for information about the proforma list records or online help. If the user provides specific matter or client information in their question, they can query for financial and budget information.

The inquiry feature can review up to 500 records at a time.

**Proforma Details**

The inquiry is available in the Proforma Details view for matter financial and budget information and online help.

**3E Proforma Help**

In addition to accessing information on matter details to assist with billing and time cards, Elite Intelligence has access to the 3E Proforma help content. You can query for "how to" information or detail specific features in the application.

## Delegate Tasks with AI for Proforma

You can instruct AI for Proforma to execute tasks such as filtering, sorting, submitting, and tracking, reducing manual intervention and improving productivity. In cases where multiple records will be affected, 3E Proforma will provide a list of records that will be changed, enabling you to de-select any items they do not want to impact before taking the action. See a list of [common actions](AI-Inquiry.md#available-actions) available for delegation below.

**Note**: This feature is only available in 3E Cloud.

Do the following to delegate tasks with AI for Proforma:

1. Click **Elite Intelligence**, on the _Proforma Li&#x73;_&#x74; _or Proforma Details_ page.
2. Enter a task for the AI to complete (e.g., close all proformas less than $1000) in the prompt field and click **Send**.
3. Do one of the following:

* **Complete Task** - Click this link to have the AI complete the task on the listed record(s).
* **Dismiss Tasks** - Click to cancel the task request.
* **Click to View All** - Click to view a list of records generated by the AI. Specify the records on which the AI will carry out the task. Click Complete Tasks

## AI for Proforma - Stock Prompt Library

AI for 3E Proforma comes with a list of pre-defined stock prompts for frequently used inquiries in the Proforma Details view. This prompt library simplifies workflows, reduce errors, and improve overall efficiency in managing proformas.

To access the library, after opening the AI Inquiry window, click **Elite Intelligence** and select **Library**. Select a listed prompt and click **Send**.
