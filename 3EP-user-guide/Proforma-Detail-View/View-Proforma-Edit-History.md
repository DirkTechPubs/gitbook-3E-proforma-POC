## View Proforma Edit History

3E Proforma includes an audit of edits made at both the proforma and card levels. The edit history is updated after a proforma has been saved.

To view edit history at the proforma level:

1.  Locate a proforma in the Proforma list.

2.  Select the **Edit History**from the Proforma-level **Action** menu. The Edit history side panel opens and edits for all editors display.

3.  Make a selection from the **Show edits by** drop-down list and choose an editor to select a specific editor’s changes, .

4.  To see a specific editor’s changes for a specific date/time, click that editor’s name.
