# Apply Funds

If the Matter record has funds in Lawyer’s Trust account(s), also known as IOLA or IOLTA bank account(s), funds in Unallocated Billed on Account (BOA), or Unallocated Credits, a Funds tab will display in the proforma details. The Funds tab has sub-tabs for each type of funds that are available to be applied to the proforma, trust and unallocated credits. For example, if the matter has funds in a Lawyer’s Trust account and Unallocated Credits there will be two (2) sub-tabs on the Funds tab. If the Matter record has more than one bank account holding trust funds, there will be a record on the Trust sub-tab for each bank account.

Do the following to apply funds:

1. Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.
2. Click the proforma number to access the Proforma Details view.
3. Click on the desired sub-tab (i.e., Trust or Unallocated credit).
4. Expand the row for the available amount to apply to the proforma when the invoice is generated.
5. Complete the required fields of information. Required fields are designated with an \* next to the field label. See [Proforma Details Form and Field Definitions](../Proforma-Details-Form-and-Field-Definitions.md#proforma-details-form-and-field-definitions) for details.
6. Click **Apply** to save and apply the funds. An _Applied_ badge displays on the funds row. Additionally, the _Available_ count decreases by one (1) count and the _Applied_ count increases by one (1) on the sub-tab.
