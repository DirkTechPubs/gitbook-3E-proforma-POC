### **Combine Cards**

Do the following to combine selected cards into a new cart.

**Note**: Whether the 3E Proforma action of Combine is automatically loaded into 3E in Proforma Edit depends on the “Commit_cardactions” setting in core 3E. If the setting is set to **False**, a biller will be required to review and finalize the Combine action in Proforma Edit by clicking on ProcTransfer or ProcExlude action. If the setting is set to **True**, the Combine action is performed automatically in 3E Proforma on the Save & recalc/Save & close/Submit actions.

1.  Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.

2.  Click the proforma number to access the Proforma Details view.

3.  Click the appropriate tab (i.e., Fees, Costs, or Charge).

4.  Select two or more cards to combine.

5.  Click the card-level **Action** menu and select **Combine**

6.  Select attributes to apply in the card resulting from the combining.

<img src="../../3ep-ug-img/media/image95.png" style="width:6.15625in;height:2.59375in" />

7.  Click **Combine**.

8.  Click **Save & Close**.
