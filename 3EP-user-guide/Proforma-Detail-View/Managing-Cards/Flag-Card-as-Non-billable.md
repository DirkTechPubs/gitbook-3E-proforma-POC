### **Flag Card as Non-billable**

Do the following to flag a card as non-billable. Depending on the card type, this action reduces the hours/units and amount to zero. In 3E, the card will be marked for Excluded and Non Billable.

**Note**: If you choose this action, you are required to enter the appropriate reason you are marking the card as non-billable in the **Comment** pop-up. This allows approvers/billers to understand why you are marking the card as non-billable.

1.  Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.

2.  Click the proforma number to access the Proforma Details view.

3.  Click the appropriate tab (i.e., Fees, Costs, or Charge)

4.  Click the card-level **Action** menu and select **Non-billable**.

5.  Type a reason for the status change.

6.  Click **Complete non-billable**.

7.  Click **Save & Close**.
