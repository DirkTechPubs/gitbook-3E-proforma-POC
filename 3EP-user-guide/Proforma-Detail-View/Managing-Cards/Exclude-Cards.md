### **Exclude Cards**

Do the following to exclude a card from a proforma:

**Note**: If a change was made to the card’s billable amounts, and then the proforma action is ‘Exclude’, the edited billable amount is cleared, and the time card displays the correct billable amount.

1.  Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.

2.  Click the proforma number to access the Proforma Details view.

3.  Click the appropriate tab (i.e., Fees, Costs, or Charge)

4.  Click the card-level **Action** menu and select **Exclude**.

5.  Click **Save & Close**.

**Note**: Whether the 3E Proforma action of Exclude is automatically loaded into 3E in Proforma Edit depends on the “Commit_cardactions” setting in core 3E. If the setting is set to **False**, a biller will be required to review and finalize the Exclude action in Proforma Edit by clicking on ProcExlude action. If the setting is set to **True**, the Exclude action is performed automatically in 3E Proforma on the Save & recalc/Save & close/Submit actions.
