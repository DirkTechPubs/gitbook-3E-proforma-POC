### **View Comments in 3E**

Proforma level comments are visible to the biller in 3E via the Comments icon.

1.  Open a proforma in the Proforma Edit process.

2.  Click the **Comments** icon in the **Action** toolbar of the proforma to view any proforma level comments.

3.  Click **Close** to exit the comments pop-up.
