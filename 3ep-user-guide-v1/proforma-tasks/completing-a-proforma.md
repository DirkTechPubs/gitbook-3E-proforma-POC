## Completing a Proforma

When you have finished your edits and you want the proforma flagged as completed so that other users on the proforma’s collaboration know your work is done, do the following:

1.  Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.

2.  Select the check box in the **Select** column for the listed proforma.

3.  Click the [<u>Proforma-level Action menu</u>](../getting-started/navigating-3e-proforma-walkthrough/navigating-the-proforma-list.md#action-menus) and select **Mark as Complete**. Any unsaved changes are automatically saved. The proforma will move from the My Proformas/Needs review/In review/Urgent list to the Completed list. A check mark will display in the Complete column of the [<u>Collaborator List</u>](../collaboration/collaborator-list-form-and-field-definitions.md#collaborator-list-form-and-field-definitions).
