## Add Invoice Narrative

Click Invoice Narrative to enter text that will display in the preface of the bill sent to the client. The invoice narrative is added in the displayed pop-up.

Do the following to add an invoice narrative:

1.  Click the number of a proforma listed in the Proforma List view.

2.  Select **Invoice Narrative** from the proforma-level **Action** menu in the Proforma Detail view.

3.  Type text in the narrative field.

4.  Click **Save**.
