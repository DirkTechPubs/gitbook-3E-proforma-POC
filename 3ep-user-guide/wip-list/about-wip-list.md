# About WIP List

The WIP list displays a list of Matters for which you are the assigned Billing Attorney, or you are working as the assigned Billing Attorney, and where the Matters have work-in-progress (WIP). You can specify criteria to determine the total amounts of WIP on the Matters that displays in the list. See the [WIP List Form and Field Definitions](wip-list-form-and-field-definitions.md#wip-list-form-and-field-definitions) for details.

The WIP list view displays for the Billing Attorney only when the organization has enabled this feature. If you do not see the WIP list, then you either do not have rights to the list or your organization has not enabled this feature.
