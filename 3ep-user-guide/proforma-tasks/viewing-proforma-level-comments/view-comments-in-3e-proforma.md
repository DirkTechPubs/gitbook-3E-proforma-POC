### **View Comments in 3E Proforma**

Do the following to view proforma-level comments in 3E Proforma:

**Note**: The existence of proforma-level comments is indicated by a dot displayed on the Proforma-level **Action** menu, either in Proforma List or Proforma Detail view.

1.  Click the number of a proforma listed in the Proforma List view.

2.  Select **Add comments** from the proforma-level **Action** menu in the Proforma Detail view. The Proforma comments pop-up displays, listing previously added comments.

3.  Click **Close**.
