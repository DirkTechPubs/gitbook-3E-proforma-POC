### **Needs review**

Selecting the **Needs review** category displays all proformas for which the user is the owner, and which are not yet worked on.

Proformas with the following statuses are available:

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 10%" />
<col style="width: 25%" />
<col style="width: 63%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr>
<th><strong>Status</strong></th>
<th><strong>Sub-status</strong></th>
<th><strong>Description</strong></th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="4"><strong>Needs review</strong></td>
<td>-</td>
<td>Proforma is waiting for the billing attorney’s review. Proforma appears on the billing attorney’s dashboard and hasn’t been touched yet.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Rejected</td>
<td>Proformas rejected by approver and returned to the billing attorney’s review.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after they were already submitted and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Biller returned</td>
<td>This status is for proformas that were returned to the billing attorney by a biller after they were already submitted, and the biller approves/makes specified edits and that were opened/edited by the billing attorney.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

 

The following actions are available depending on proforma status/sub-status:

<colgroup>

<tr>
<td><strong>Print Proforma</strong></td>

<p> </p></td>
<td>Select this action to view a PDF version of the proforma</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>Bill Preview</strong></td>

<p> </p></td>

</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>Close</strong></td>
<td><p>Needs review/all sub-statuses</p>
<p> </p></td>

<p><strong>Note</strong>: Close is only available to Proforma Owners.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>Submit</strong></td>
<td><p>Needs review/all sub-statuses</p>
<p> </p></td>

<tr>
<td><strong>Forward</strong></td>
<td><p>Needs review/all sub-statuses</p>
<p> </p></td>

</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>Defer</strong></td>
<td><p>Needs review/all sub-statuses</p>

<tr>
<td><strong>Set priority</strong></td>
<td><p>Needs review/all sub-statuses</p>
<p> </p></td>

</tr>
<tr>
<td><strong>Comments</strong></td>
<td><p>Needs review/all sub-statuses</p>
<p> </p></td>
<td>Select this action to view/add proforma-level comments.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
