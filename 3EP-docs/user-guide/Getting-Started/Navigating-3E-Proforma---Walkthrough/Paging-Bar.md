### **Paging Bar**

Paging options are available in the Proforma List view and in card lists in the Proforma Details view. The paging bar is located at the bottom of the page.

<img src="../../3EP-UG-img/media/image21.png" style="width:6.5in;height:1.92708in" />

 

Use the Paging bar to do the following

- **Next / Previous Page** - Click the **Next** or **Previous** arrows to move between pages in a list.

- **Jump to Page** - In a long list, click the number of the page shown on Paging bar or enter a specific page number and click **Go**.

- **Set Page Lengt**h - Select a value from the drop-down list on the Paging bar to specify how many rows or records (e.g., proformas or cards) to include on a page.

- **Jump to Top** - Click **Back to top** to jump to the top of the currently viewed page.
