## About 3E Proforma

The traditional proforma process is inherently cumbersome and time consuming. By eliminating the reams of paper that must be circulated between the Accounting staff and the timekeepers, and transforming handwritten changes, notations, and explanations to typewritten information, 3E Proforma provides a unique solution that simplifies, expedites, and mobilizes the proforma process. It's the fastest way to route proformas, the best way to ensure that instructions and mark-ups are both readable and implemented accurately, and the most efficient method for processing proformas.
