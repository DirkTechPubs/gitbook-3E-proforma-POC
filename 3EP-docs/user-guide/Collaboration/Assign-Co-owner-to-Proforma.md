## Assign Co-owner to Proforma

Proforma owners have the ability to assign a co-owner. The co-owner has the same rights as the proforma owner, allowing for shared proforma responsibilities. Assigning a co-owner to a proforma requires the following steps:

- Designate a co-owner in 3E (i.e., at the matter, client, timekeeper, or proforma level)

- Assign a [<u>collaborator</u>](../Collaboration.md#collaboration) as a co-owner in 3E Proforma

- Modify 3E Proforma stock workflow configurations in 3E

Once these steps have been completed, both the owner and the co-owner will receive the proforma and have owner rights to edit and submit the proforma in 3E Proforma.
