### **Write Down a Card**

Do the following to write-down the amount of a card. The action reduces the card's hours/units - depends on the card type, and amount to zero. In 3E, the card **Display** check box will be deselected and **Excluded** check box will remain deselected.

**Note**: If you choose **this** action, you are required to enter the appropriate reason for the write down in the **Comment** pop-up. This allows approvers /billers to understand why you are writing down the time card.

1.  Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.

2.  Click the proforma number to access the Proforma Details view.

3.  Click the appropriate tab (i.e., Fees, Costs, or Charge)

4.  Click the card-level **Action** menu and select **Write down**.

5.  Type a reason for the status change.

6.  Click **Complete write down**.

7.  Click **Save & Close**.
