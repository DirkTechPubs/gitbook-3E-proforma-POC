### **Remove Applied Funds**

Do the following to un-apply trust or credit funds:

1.  Locate the proforma containing funds on the Proforma list.

2.  Click the proforma number to access the Proforma Details view.

3.  Click on the desired sub-tab (i.e., Trust or Unallocated credit).

4.  Click the **X** on the *Applied* badge. the “Available” count increases by one (1) and the “Applied” count decreases by one (1) on the sub-tab.
