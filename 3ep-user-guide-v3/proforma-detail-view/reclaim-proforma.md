# Reclaim Proforma

You can reclaim a proforma that was submitted to the Accounting department in error. However, you must reclaim the proforma before it has been opened by the Accounting department or invoiced.

Do the following to reclaim a proforma:

1. Click the **Completed proformas** or the **Approval pending** categories in the Proforma List view.
2. Click the number of a submitted proforma in the list.
3. Select **Reclaim** from the proforma-level **Action** menu in the Proforma Detail view.
