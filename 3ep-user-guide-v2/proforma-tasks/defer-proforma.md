## Defer Proforma

Proformas can be deferred to exclude them being billed during a billing cycle. Deferred proformas move to the completed proformas list.

**Note**: Deferred proformas will remain on the Completed proformas list for the period of time designated by setups in 3E or until a new proforma for the Matter is generated.

Do the following to defer a proforma:

1.  Locate the proforma that's ready for review in the [<u>Proforma list</u>](../getting-started/navigating-3e-proforma-walkthrough.md#navigating-3e-proforma---walkthrough).

2.  Select the **Defer** from the Proforma-level **Action** menu. Any unsaved changes are automatically saved and the proforma is deferred to the Accounting department. Deferring a proforma removes it from the proforma list.

**Note**: To defer a batch of proformas for review, select proformas displayed in the Proforma List and then select **Defer** from the [<u>Proforma List Action menu</u>](../getting-started/navigating-3e-proforma-walkthrough/navigating-the-proforma-list.md#action-menus).

**Note**: Proformas can also be deferred in the Proforma Details view. Select **Defer** from the [<u>Proforma-level</u> **Action** <u>menu</u>](../getting-started/navigating-3e-proforma-walkthrough/navigating-the-proforma-detail-view.md#navigating-the-proforma-detail-view).
