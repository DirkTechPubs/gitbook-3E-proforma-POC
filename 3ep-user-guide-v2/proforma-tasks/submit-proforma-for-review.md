## Submit Proforma for Review

After reviewing a proforma, making any necessary changes and adding instructions for the Accounting staff, the proforma needs to be sent to the Accounting department for review.

Do the following to submit proformas for review:

1.  Locate the proforma that's ready for review in the Proforma list.

2.  Select the **Submit** from the Proforma-level **Action** menu. Any unsaved changes are automatically saved and the proforma is submitted to the Accounting department. Submitting a proforma moves it to the Approval pending or Completed proformas list, depending on whether approval is required

**Note**: To submit a batch of proformas for review, see [Batch Processing Proforma Actions](batch-processing-proforma-actions.md#batch-processing-proforma-actions) for details.

**Note**: Proformas can also be submitted in the Proforma Details view. Select **Submit** from the [<u>Proforma-level</u> **Action** <u>menu</u>](../getting-started/navigating-3e-proforma-walkthrough/navigating-the-proforma-detail-view.md#navigating-the-proforma-detail-view).
