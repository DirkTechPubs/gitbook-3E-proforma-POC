## About Proforma Details

The Proforma Details view is used to view proforma header information and complete tasks such as reviewing and editing time, cost, and charge cards.

Access the proforma details by clicking on the Proforma number on the Proforma list. By default, you will be taken to the Fees details.

**Note**: When the proforma details are opened, it becomes locked. Other users will be able to access the proforma in read-only view. If a user opens proforma details when the proforma is locked by another user, a warning message displays.
