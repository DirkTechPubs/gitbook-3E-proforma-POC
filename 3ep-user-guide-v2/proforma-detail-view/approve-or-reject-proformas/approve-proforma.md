### **Approve Proforma**

Do the following to approve a proforma

1.  Click on the number of a proforma displayed in the Proforma list to access the proforma details.

2.  Review the changes that have been made to the proforma.

3.  Select **Approve** from the **Proforma Action** menu. A message displays that "Proforma XXXX has been approved." The proforma is removed from the list of *Needs review* and is sent to the Billing department.
