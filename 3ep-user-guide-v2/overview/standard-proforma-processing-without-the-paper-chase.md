## Standard Proforma Processing Without the Paper Chase

3E Proforma transforms the traditional method of routing and marking up proforma printouts into a paperless process that can be completed either in the office or from a remote location. 3E Proforma mirrors accepted proforma mark-up conventions, eliminating the need for Accounting staff and timekeepers to learn a new system. Users simply log on to view their proformas. Mark-ups and annotations previously made by hand can now be executed online.
